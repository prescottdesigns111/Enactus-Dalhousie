The value of partnering with Enactus Dalhousie

By supporting Enactus Dalhousie, your organization becomes part of a movement that empowers students to turn innovative ideas into real-world impact while gaining meaningful visibility within our community.

Sponsorship Tiers

Find the right fit for your organization

Each tier is designed to deliver meaningful value to your brand while powering student-led impact in Halifax.

Diamond
$7,500

The most exposure to students seeking co-op and full-time opportunities. Diamond Sponsors gain exclusive opportunity to work alongside Enactus for the 2026/2027 school year.

  • Title recognition as Presenting Sponsor of the Enactus Dalhousie Gala
  • Opening remarks (15-minute speaking time)
  • Up to 4 representatives at networking sessions
  • Premium logo placement on all event materials, social media & presentations
  • Recognition during the event & in all promotional communications
  • Company materials / promo items at the event
  • Priority push of job postings to students in select programs
  • Mentorship or advisory session with one project team
  • Featured in Post-Event Impact Report
Platinum
$5,000

High-profile exposure for organizations looking to connect with emerging talent and support community innovation.

  • Keynote speaker slot (10-minute speaking time)
  • Up to 3 representatives at networking sessions
  • Dedicated company recognition during the gala
  • Featured sponsor spotlight on our social media
  • Company promotional materials at the event
  • Priority push of job postings to students in select programs
  • Featured in Post-Event Impact Report
Gold
$3,000

Excellent option for companies looking for high publicity and recognition across the Enactus Canada Network.

  • Logo placement on event materials & promotional content
  • Recognition as a Social Impact Partnership during the event
  • Up to 2 company representatives at networking sessions
  • Company promotional materials at the event
  • Priority push of job postings to students in select programs
  • Recognition in Post-Event Impact Report
Silver
$1,000

Unparalleled visibility for businesses looking to make an impact and contribute to student-led initiatives.

  • Up to 2 company representatives at networking sessions
  • Recognition as a Social Impact Partnership during the event
  • Company logo on select event materials
  • Access to the resume book
  • Recognition in Post-Event Impact Report
